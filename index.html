Share Your Need

As a K-12 teacher, you want to make "real-world" learning come alive in your class. Find community members ready to share their expertise, experiences, time, or items with your students. There are businesses, organizations, professionals, academics, parents, and young adults who would love to contribute. Reach out to them today!

3 Simple Ways to Help

1
Volunteers

Spend a little time helping teachers get things done -- from chaperones on a field-trip to helping out in the school gym -- volunteers help make things happen.

2
Experts

Think about the person who inspired you as a kid. Be that inspiration, and mentor local students today!

3
Stuff

You would be amazed at the opportunities teachers see for education in your stuff, the stuff that has been sitting there in storage for years. Give your local teachers the stuff they need to engage their students.
